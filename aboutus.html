About Us

History

JOBHUB began in co-founder Reid Hoffman's living room in 2002 and was officially launched on May 5, 2003. Today, JOBHUB leads a diversified business with revenues from membership subscriptions, advertising sales and recruitment solutions under the leadership of Ryan Roslansky.

Our Vision

The mission of JOBHUB is simple: connect the world’s professionals to make them more productive and successful.

Future Goal

Create economic opportunity for every member of the global workforce.
